Hos alogistos est'aner, hos mete phylaken ton anankaion echei, met',an atychesei eis ta koina tou biou, epamphiesai dynaito touto chremasin, all'en akalyptoi kai talaiporoi bioi cheimazomenos zei ton men aniairon echon to meros hapanton, ton d'agathon ouden meros. Hyper gar henos algon hapantas noutheto. 21 Ad horum autem sinceritatem veritatemque verborum an adspiraverit Caecilius, consideremus.
